The Paradox of Paradise

A different take of the beaches of Puerto Rico



It's been almost a year since Hurricane Maria slammed into Puerto Rico. There are still people without a safe roof over their heads and electricity in their houses. However, islanders will always find the beach to be the place where they can let go of all worries and enjoy quality time.

Beaches in Puerto Rico are a main attraction for foreigners and locals since they are warm all year round. Yet one cannot forget that the environment impacts of a category four hurricane does not disappear in a short period of time. On the island all the rivers went out of their channel and dragged debris and dead animals for months. This project intends to answer the question that locals and visitors have: Are the Puerto Rican beaches in a worst state after Maria?
